Site Plan: Music Studio

Site Name

Harmony Studios

Reason: This name was chosen because it evokes a sense of energy and passion associated with music. The "Crimson Wave" imagery suggests a powerful, flowing sound, while "Studios" clearly indicates the nature of the business. It's memorable and aligns with our reddish color scheme.

Site Purpose

The Harmony Studios website will serve as a comprehensive platform to showcase the studio's services, state-of-the-art equipment, and featured artists. It will provide detailed information about the facilities, booking options, and serve as a digital portfolio to attract new clients and collaborators in the music industry. The site aims to convey the studio's professional capabilities while also capturing the creativity and passion inherent in music production.

Scenarios

  1. What types of recording services does Harmony Studios offer, and what are their rates?
  2. Can I see examples of previous work or a list of artists who have recorded at this studio?
  3. What specific recording and mixing equipment is available at Harmony Studios?

Color Scheme

Dark Red (#8B0000): For main headings and primary accents.

Crimson (#DC143C): For secondary headings and interactive elements.

Misty Rose (#FFE4E1): For background of certain sections.

White (#FFFFFF): Main background and text on dark backgrounds.

Dark Gray (#333333): For main text on light backgrounds.

Typography

Montserrat: For headings and titles. This sans-serif font provides a modern, clean look that complements the professional image of the studio.

Open Sans: For body text and general content. This highly readable sans-serif font pairs well with Montserrat and ensures good legibility across devices.

Playfair Display: For special accents or quotes. This serif font can be used sparingly to add a touch of elegance and contrast to the overall design.
